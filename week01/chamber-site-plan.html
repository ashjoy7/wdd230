Site Plan for Huachuca City Chamber of Commerce

Site Name:

Huachuca City Chamber of Commerce

The site name includes "Chamber" to indicate its affiliation and "Huachuca City" to specify the location, ensuring relevance to the local community.

Site Purpose:

  • To promote local businesses and economic development in Huachuca City.
  • To provide resources and support for businesses and residents of Huachuca City.

Scenarios:

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?
  • What attractions or landmarks can visitors explore in Huachuca City?

Color Schema:

  • Primary Color: Blue - Used for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • Secondary Color: Orange - Used for backgrounds and accents.

Typography:

  • Heading Font: Courgette - Used for main headings.
  • Body Font: Roboto - Used for body text.
